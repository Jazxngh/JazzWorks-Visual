About Me

I’m a Software Engineering student with a passion for visual and cinematic storytelling through photography. While I love code and technology, I’m also equally drawn to capturing urban landscapes, portraits, and experimental photography in my spare time alongside with fitness while I’m currently studying Software Engineering at Sheffield Hallam University, I spend much of my time exploring the worlds of fitness, technology, and visual storytelling.
