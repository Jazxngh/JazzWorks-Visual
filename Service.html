What I offer

Jazzworks-visuals offer a variety of customizable photography packages to meet your needs, whether you're searching for standard portraits, party photography, or an event memory. Sessions are intended to be shot steadily, smooth, and produce excellent results with great attention to detail in both shooting and editing side of photography.

Session Packages

We as a brand offer two photography sessions tailored to your needs. The Standard Session which is priced at £100 includes a full shoot with unlimited high-quality photographs provided digitally in FHD via emails, galleries etc. For those looking for premium results, the Premium Session is available at£160 and includes everything of the standard package with additions such as professional retouch, colour grading and so many more. Optionally there are bonus add-ons which varies from a USB stick delivery for an another £15 to extra locations within the local area for £10, and a 1-week priority delivery option for £5. All sessions are tailored to you and is flexible and final quotes can vary depending on travel, session, and project requirements and many more...

Our sessions typically varies hour wise and include travel within the Doncaster/Sheffield area. For locations outside this range-additional charges may apply. Availability can vary depending on the season, so it's best to book in advance to grab your preferred date.
